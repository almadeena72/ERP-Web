Admission Requirements

Students seeking admission to AL MADEENA INTEGRATED CAMPUS should meet the following requirements.

📋 Eligibility

  • Age should meet the course requirement.
  • Previous academic qualification.
  • Basic interview for selected programmes.
  • Good moral character.

📄 Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card Copy
  • Passport Size Photos
  • Previous Mark Sheet
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)

📝 Admission Process

  • Fill Online Application
  • Document Verification
  • Interview / Assessment
