OUR LEADERSHIP

Meet Our Leadership

DR.FAROOQ NAEEMI AL'BUKHARI

Chairman

Chairman Message

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to AL MADEENA INTEGRATED CAMPUS, an institution committed to nurturing young minds through the integration of Islamic values and modern education. At Al Madeena, we believe that true education goes beyond academic achievement. Our mission is to develop students who are intellectually capable, morally upright, spiritually conscious, and socially responsible. We strive to create an environment where knowledge, faith, character, and leadership grow together. Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to ensure that every student receives quality education, personal guidance, and opportunities for holistic development. By combining Islamic teachings with contemporary academic excellence, we aim to prepare our students to face the challenges of the modern world while remaining firmly rooted in their faith and values. I express my sincere gratitude to our parents, teachers, well-wishers, and supporters for their continued trust and cooperation. Together, we can build a generation that contributes positively to society and serves humanity with wisdom, integrity, and compassion. May Allah (SWT) bless our efforts and grant success to all our students. Wassalamu Alaikum Warahmatullahi Wabarakatuh. Chairman AL MADEENA INTEGRATED CAMPUS
